About Us

Casas Brush is an experienced family owned and operated small business, that specializes in working on a one-to-one basis with homeowners to help bring out the beauty in every room.

Whatever your painting need--whether it's a consultation for something new and original, an updated look for an existing space, or something otherworldly altogether--we can help you get there.

We take pride our in painting and service all of the Tri-State area. We provide a 100% satisfaction guarantee. Thank you for your time and interest in our small business.
